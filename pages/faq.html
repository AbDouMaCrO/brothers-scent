Everything you need to know about ordering, delivery, payment, and our wholesale programme.

Ordering

The minimum order is 10 units for Starter accounts. Pro (Distributor) accounts start at 50 units, with volume discounts applied automatically. Enterprise accounts have no fixed minimum — we tailor each order to your needs.

Browse our catalogue, add items to your cart, then proceed to checkout — this generates a WhatsApp message with your full order. Our team confirms availability, sends you an invoice, and dispatches after payment.

Yes — you can mix any fragrance, brand, or size to reach your minimum order tier. The 10-unit minimum applies to the total order, not per SKU.

Yes. Wholesale prices are hidden for guest visitors. Register for a free account, get approved by our team (usually within 24h), and you'll see full pricing upon login.

Products

100% authentic. Every fragrance is sourced directly from brand-authorised distributors or directly from the houses themselves. We do not deal in grey-market, replica, or tester units sold as retail.

The majority of our catalogue is 100ml (the most popular wholesale size). Some brands also offer 50ml and 200ml. Contact us for specific size availability on any SKU.

Our catalogue is built from Fragrantica's top-ranked fragrances across UAE and Saudi Arabia — the most-searched and most-rated scents in the Gulf region, curated for wholesale market demand. We refresh it regularly as trends shift.

Delivery

We ship across the GCC: UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, and Oman. For orders outside the GCC, contact us directly to discuss shipping options and rates.

UAE: same day or next day. Saudi Arabia: 1–2 business days. Kuwait & Qatar: 2–3 business days. Bahrain & Oman: 3–5 business days. All orders are packed and dispatched within 24h of payment confirmation.

Yes. We send you a tracking number via WhatsApp as soon as the order is dispatched. You can track in real-time through the carrier's website.

We accept bank transfer (preferred for large orders), and offer Net-15 / Net-30 payment terms for Pro and Enterprise accounts. All payments are confirmed before dispatch.

Account & Registration

Click "Register" on the homepage, fill in your name, company, email, and phone. Our team reviews your application — legitimate business buyers are approved within 24 hours.

We don't require documentation to register, but we do manually review each account. We prioritise verified retailers, boutiques, and established distributors. If you're a new business, include a brief note about your operation when you register.
