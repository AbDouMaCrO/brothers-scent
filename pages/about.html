WHO WE ARE

Built for wholesale, born in the Gulf

BrotherScents was founded by fragrance enthusiasts who saw a gap in the Gulf wholesale market — buyers struggled to source authentic, premium perfumes at competitive bulk prices without flying to trade shows or dealing with unreliable distributors.

We built direct relationships with fragrance houses in France, the UAE, and Saudi Arabia — brands like Lattafa, Afnan, Al Haramain, and international names like Dior and Creed — so our clients skip the middleman entirely.

Today we serve perfume retailers, boutiques, and resellers across the GCC with a catalogue of over 500 authentic fragrances and same-week dispatch.

100% Authentic

Every bottle sourced directly from brand-authorised distributors. No grey market, no fakes.

Wholesale Pricing

Tiered pricing structures for retailers, boutiques, and large distributors — the more you buy, the less you pay per unit.

Fast Dispatch

Orders confirmed via WhatsApp, packed, and shipped within 24–48 hours across the GCC.
