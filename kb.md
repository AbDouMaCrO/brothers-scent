## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I order as an individual (not a business)?**
A: BrotherScents is a wholesale platform for businesses. Minimum order is 10 units. Individual retail orders are not our core business.

**Q: How do I see prices?**
A: Register an account, wait for admin approval, then log in. Prices are hidden from unapproved/logged-out users.

**Q: What is the minimum order?**
A: 10 units minimum for Starter tier. Units can be mixed across different products and brands.

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Typically 1–3 business days within UAE. Other GCC countries 3–7 business days depending on destination.

**Q: Are the products authentic?**
A: Yes. 100% authentic, sourced from brands or authorised distributors. No counterfeits.

**Q: Can I order a specific fragrance not on your website?**
A: Possibly. Contact us on WhatsApp with the product details — we may be able to source it.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss payment options for your order size.

**Q: Do you ship outside the GCC?**
A: Our primary coverage is GCC. Contact us for international shipping enquiries.

**Q: Can I return products?**
A: Returns are handled case by case. Contact us within 48 hours of receiving an order if there is an issue.

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: Once dispatched, tracking details are shared via WhatsApp.

**Q: Do you have a physical store?**
A: We operate as a wholesale distributor based in Dubai. No retail walk-in store.

**Q: What are your best-selling products?**
A: Top movers: Lattafa Khamrah, Armaf Club De Nuit Intense, Dior Sauvage, Chanel Bleu, Rasasi Hawas, Al Haramain Amber Oud.

**Q: Do you offer gift sets?**
A: Yes — gift sets available especially for Eid, Ramadan, and National Day seasons. Ask for seasonal stock lists.

**Q: What is oud?**
A: Oud (agarwood) is a precious resinous wood used in Arabic perfumery. It produces a rich, smoky, complex scent highly prized in GCC culture. We carry pure oud oils and oud-based perfumes from top Arabic houses.
